Meet our Team

Stefan Hochhuth

Founder at Hochhuth Consulting GmbH

Stefan is an economist by trade, holding master's degrees in both Economics and Social/Political Sciences. He has a particular interest in topics related to digital governance, political economy, and economic policy. With over 20 years of experience in international development cooperation, he has worked for various institutions, including GIZ, SDC, and the World Bank. Recognizing the potential of digitalization for poverty reduction, he bridges the gap between development economics/governance and digital solutions knowledge.

Robert Ungethuem

Lead Digital Governance Specialist

Robert is a development economist who has spent most of his work at the intersection of international development cooperation and digitalization. Before joining Hochhuth Consulting, Robert guided a network of financial sector NGOs in Southern Africa to digitize their operations. As a consultant Robert has worked with the GIZ, SDC and others to develop smart digital processes.

THORDIS JACOBS

Senior Digital Governance Specialist

Thordis has a degree in economics who has extensive experience in the fields of research, political consulting and networking, mostly as an assistant to the management. As an all-rounder, she is familiar with administrative and change management procedures and processes. Throughout her career Thordis has always had a fable for digitalization and in particular digital communication methods.

ALINA CHYHASOVA

UI/UX Designer

Alina is an UI/UX designer at Hochhuth Consulting, where she focuses on improving the interface and user interaction with the platform. Her professional approach is rooted in continuous improvement and the search for innovative solutions to enhance the user experience. She holds a degree in Management of International business and economics from the Kyiv National Economics University in Ukraine, a country currently going through challenging times, yet marked by the courage and determination of its citizens.

Mattis Hahn-Temba

Data Specialist

Benefits

Purpose-Driven

We strongly believe that life is about making an impact and that everyone can make an impact. Working for Hochhuth Consulting we believe that our workplace must follow this idea too in the way we conduct business and in the way we work with our partners and employees.

Work from the most beautiful places

Since all of us are in some form involved in international development, we have all gotten a weakness for travelling the world. This is also supported in our way of work. Using our task management allows us to be flexible in the way when and where we do our work. 

Supporting your career development

You never stop learning. It is fascinating to see from which professions people come from and diverse backgrounds they bring to the table. To facilitate this movement continuous learning whether it be a formal education or informally is extremely important. We would like to support this through flexible working conditions and opportunities.

Embracing Our Corporate Culture

Collaboration Trip to France

In 2021, new opportunities had piled up, so we decided to take all the tenders to the tracks. By train we travelled down to the south of France where we undertook workshops, discussions and reviews along the way. Of courseit’s not all work and we get to see some sights and do the occasional hike.

Strategy Workshopping

With the help of strategic thinker and designer, Christoph, we embarked on a day of exercise to explore what our service portfolio looks like and what makes our services unique. Given our diverse nature and all the different minds and backgrounds, this is a great opportunity to align all our thinking.

Poland Retreat

Once a year Hochhuth Consulting goes on a retreat bringing all its staff back together. 2022 with Leonie just having joined the band, we went on to the cold Baltic Coast of Poland. In between strategic workshopping and onboardings, we did manage to take some steps on the beach and visit the local spa.
